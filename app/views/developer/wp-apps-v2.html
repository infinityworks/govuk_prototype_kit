How will my app be appraised?

NHS Approved

An app meets NHS quality standards for clinical effectiveness, safety, usability and accessibility and has a supportive evidence base

Being tested in the NHS

An app meets NHS quality standards for safety and usability and is being tested now with NHS patients to see if there is sufficient evidence to provide them with an NHS stamp of approval
