Submit Your App

Your app must already be in public use before it can be submitted for pre-assessment by NHS Digital to determine suitability. After that, you will be invited to answer our Digital Assessment Questions (DAQs) to help us ensure that only trusted, high-quality apps are published on our library.

Your answers will be reviewed by NHS subject matter experts or our assessment partners from fields including clinical effectiveness, data security and usability, and you may be asked to provide more information to complete this process.

Once your app has met the assessment criteria, it will be published on our Apps Library within the NHS.uk website. The site receives around 48 million visits a month from members of the public and healthcare professionals.

We are currently prioritising the assessment of apps that address the key health themes of social care, cancer, mental health, maternity and long-term conditions like diabetes and asthma. However, submitting your details will mean you are included on our database and will be contacted when the health priority matches the type of app youâ€™re offering.
